3rd Annual Global Soft Toy Competition Offical Rules

1
The Competition is open to little artists,who have great imagination and fanciful dreams of soft little friends.
2
Drawings must be original creations and must be created by a child of age 0-12.
3 Drawings must be submitted by the artist's legal parent/guardian who is over the age of 18.
4 Drawings can be submitted either online or in-store.
5 Drawings submitted in-store as part of the contest will not be returned.
6 Artists are required to have fun!
The deadline for drawing submissions is 10/30 at HH:MM:SS EST
